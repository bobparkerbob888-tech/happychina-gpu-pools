How to Connect

1

Get a PPC Address

Download the Peercoin wallet and generate a receiving address. This is where your block rewards will be sent.

2

Configure Your Miner

Works with all SHA-256 miners — from USB miners to S21s. Starting difficulty is 10K with automatic vardiff that scales to your hashrate.

URL: stratum+tcp://<your-umbrel-ip>:2028 Worker: <your PPC address>.<worker name> Password: x
3

Start Mining

Vardiff starts at 10,000 and automatically adjusts to match your miner. Small miners (1 TH/s) and large miners (400+ TH/s) are both welcome. This is a solo pool — when you find a block, the entire reward goes to your address. Use the miner lookup above to monitor your workers.

About Solo Mining

In solo mining, you compete to find blocks independently. There is no reward sharing — if your miner solves a block, you receive the full block reward. The tradeoff is higher variance: it may take longer between payouts, but each payout is the full block reward.

Peercoin uses SHA-256 proof-of-work with a target block time of ~10 minutes, similar to Bitcoin. Any SHA-256 ASIC miner (e.g., Antminer S19, S21) is compatible.
