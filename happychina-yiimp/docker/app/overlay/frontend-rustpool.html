Getting Started

Everything you need to start mining on HappyChina Pool

🚀 Quick Overview

HappyChina Pool is a Scrypt mining pool running on Yiimp. We support merge mining, meaning when you mine Litecoin (the parent chain), you automatically earn rewards on all auxiliary chains (DOGE, BELLS, JKC, PEPE, LKY, DINGO, TRMP) at no extra cost or hashrate.

No registration required. Your Litecoin wallet address is your username.

⚙ Stratum Connection Details

Choose the port that best matches your mining hardware:

🖥 ASIC Miner Configuration

For Bitmain L-series ASICs (L3+, L7, L9), enter these settings in your miner's web interface:

Pool URL: stratum+tcp://YOUR_UMBREL_IP:3332 Worker: YOUR_LTC_ADDRESS.worker1 Password: c=LTC

Replace YOUR_LTC_ADDRESS with your Litecoin wallet address. The .worker1 suffix is optional and helps you identify individual miners on the dashboard.

If you prefer auto-tuning instead of fixed diff, use 3333. For very large rentals, step up to 3335, 3334, or 3331 depending on the starting difficulty you want.

💰 Payouts

Payouts are processed automatically by the pool. Each coin is paid out to the wallet address you mine with. For merge-mined coins, you'll need to set up wallet addresses through the Yiimp web interface.

  • Pool fee: 0% by default
  • Minimum payout: 0.001 (per coin)
  • Payouts run on a regular schedule
  • Check your balance: Dashboard page with your wallet address

⛏ Merge Mining Explained

Merge mining (also called Auxiliary Proof-of-Work) allows mining multiple blockchains simultaneously without any additional hashrate. Litecoin serves as the parent chain, and the auxiliary chains piggyback on its proof of work.

When the pool finds a valid share for LTC, it checks if that same work is also valid for the auxiliary chains. If it meets the difficulty target of an aux chain, a block is found on that chain too. This is why you earn multiple coins for the price of one.

LTC - Litecoin (Parent) DOGE - Dogecoin BELLS - Bells JKC - Junkcoin PEPE - Pepecoin LKY - Luckycoin DINGO - Dingocoin TRMP - Trumpow
